RoadMap

  • 2018

  • The 1st quarter
    SMS PAYMENT
    • Pay for goods and services with SMS (Croatia only)
    • Users can enter the bill number, merchant id and bill total, send that information via SMS or via the application. The bill total is deducted from their phone bill.
  • The 2nd quarter
    ORDERING USING QR CODES
    • Ordering system that uses specific QR codes placed on tables identifying each one
    • User scans the QR code and gets access to merchants menu of items and services, which user can order to his table and pay for via SMS.
  • The 3rd quarter
    Purchasing Radio/TV/Newspaper Advertisements
    • Advertisements can now be ordered and paid for via SEND2Pay advertisement publishing service.
    • Users fill a simple form with necessary information about their ad and pay via SMS.
    Credit Card Payments
    • The most anticipated feature for our service. Users can pay with credit cards for every SEND2Pay service. With minimal fees, incredible speed and impenetrable security.
  • The 4th quarter
    Fiscalization
    • This is a huge deal and a game changer for small merchants and seasonal workers in Croatia.
    • This feature gives every user an option to become a merchant and issue receipts with minimal effort and investment.
    • Small businesses will benefit the most because they can accept credit card payments without having to go through the long process of dealing with banks and card issuers.
    Waiter/Manager Specific Application
    • SEND2Pay application specifically for waiters and managers of catering trade.
    • Waiters can make orders, manage tables with our table and order system, manage their working hours and payrolls, see analytics, have direct communication with every guest via the SEND2Pay app.

  • 2019

  • The 1st quarter
    Loyalty Program
    • Product/Merchant loyalty scheme for rewarding loyal and returning customers.
    • Management of special events, happy hour promotions, advertisements and freebies. Have complex rewarding systems set up in minutes. Coupons, vouchers, discounts, BOGOF...
    Manager Back-Office
    • Back-office web service for managing your business, with a ton of features. Maintaining your business will be easier than ever.
  • The 2nd quarter
    Waiter/Managet Application Modules
    • Custom made modules for specific use cases that will improve your business in many ways
    • Modules such as: Hotel specific ordering cases, sport events, food and drink ordering and payment solutions
  • The 3rd quarter
    Manager Back-Office modules
    • Custom made modules for specific use cases that will improve overview and management of your business.
    • Specifically, monitor each staff member, item/product management (description, images, allergens, sizes). Location based notifications for subscribed customers. A more indepth loyalty system.
